Tier 1

Grunt

Grunts, which are common in large numbers due to the fact that they are easy for the alien parasite to incubate.

Stealth is recommended to eliminate with more efficiency those targets

Sprawl

Sprawl,are part of the ecosystem in infected areas,they can slow down operators make then more vulnerable and make enemies faster in that area but they can be easily destroyed .

Is recommended to destroy the Sprawl before it spreads.

Nest

Nests, They look harmless but when enemies are alerted around the nests thoses will summon more enemies

Targeting a nest and not allerting the enemies when near the nest its the best way to avoid a fight

Tier 2

Rooter

Rooters, can smash the ground in a line and Root your allies, making then unnable to move and more vulnerable to take damage.

Hit the enemy in the back its the only way to free your allies from the Rooter

Breacher

Breachers,when alerted they will charge onto the operator , when reached the operator they can explode doing a massive area damage.

The best way to eliminate the breacher it's using long range strategies.

Spiker

Spikers, can shoot armour piercing rounds doing massive damage on the operator.

It's recommended to kill the Spikers ASAP, once they can do a lot of damage in a short amount of time

Tier 3

Smasher

Smashers,charge towards the operator doing damage and knocking then back.He has a though carapace that is almost bulletproof.

It's recommended make hits in the back, the only place he hasn't a carapace.

Lurker

Lurkers, can make another grunts invisbles making more difficult to avoid certain types of enemies.

It's recommended to kill a Lurker quickly to avoid difficult in combat

Tier 4

Tormentor

Rooters, shoots projectiles which inflict a high damage and has a fast mobility using even the sprawl to teleport between areas.

It's recommended stay together and work together.
